How it works

People spend 80-90% of their time in indoor environments such as offices, undergrounds, shopping malls and airports. On the other hand, the uptake of interesting applications in indoor spaces (e.g., navigation, inventory management and elderly support) has so far been hampered by the lack of technologies that can provide indoor location (position) accurately, in real-time, in an energy-efficient manner and without expensive additional hardware.

Anyplace deploys a number of innovative concepts, including crowdsourcing, big-data management, energy-aware processing, multi-device optimization and mobile data management, in order to realize a power-efficient indoor localization and navigation technology that recently won several international research awards for its accuracy (less than 1.96 meters)!

About

Anyplace is a first-of-a-kind indoor information service offering GPS-less localization, navigation and search inside buildings using ordinary smartphones.

Anyplace has been developed by researchers and students at the Data Management Systems Laboratory (DMSL), Department of Computer Science at the University of Cyprus.

To get a quote on how to enable advanced mapping and navigation in your building drop us a line here or at anyplace@cs.ucy.ac.cy. You can additionally check out our Web 2.0 API under the Developer section.

An open source version of the Anyplace localization subsystem, developed in collaboration with the KIOS Research Center at the University of Cyprus, is available under a GNU GPL licence with the name Airplace. To download Airplace please click here.
